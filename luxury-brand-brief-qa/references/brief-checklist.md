# Brief Checklist

## Scoring Model (0-3)

Use 0 for missing, 1 for weak, 2 for usable, 3 for strong.

## Dimensions

- Business objective clarity
- Audience precision and ranking
- Offer and conversion path clarity
- Competitive differentiation
- Brand voice consistency
- Visual territory constraints
- Content and asset readiness
- Delivery scope realism
- Timeline and stakeholder alignment
- Risk visibility

## Red Flags

- "Luxury" stated without proof points
- Too many equal-priority audiences
- No primary conversion event
- Tone mismatch between product and language
- Unbounded scope with fixed deadline
